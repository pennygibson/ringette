Beausejour Ringers Ringette

Play Ringette in Beausejour

We are proud to offer this amazing sport to athletes of all ages!!!

The season typically runs from October - March.

Our teams practice once a week with games on the weekends. Dependant on the coach and parents involved you may play in tournaments throughout the season.

Ringette in our community has been proven to be fun, fast and forever. The athletes work hard and are dedicated. They show us what real teamwork is all about!!!

Please email beausejourringers@hotmail.ca or use the contact form below to sign up today!!!
